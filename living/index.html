CONCEPT STATEMENT

Be-Living is a online communication platform which is a place to display the design works by interior designers and the consumer can choose their loved interior designer.

ROLE

UX Design

PRODUCT DEFINITION

With the upgrade of consumption, more and more people are beginning to show their individual needs for their home decoration. At this time, if there is an effective communication platform to establish the connection between consumers and designers，the consumers can quickly find their favorite decorative style. Therefore, this product is defined as a communication platform between consumers and designers.

USER ANALYZE

Through the investigation of target users, most of them wanted to have their special style of their home decoration. In addition, the interior designer wanna have a platform to show the design ability. Therefore, they need a platform to interact their idea. According to the user’s characteristics, both of them have high quality of life.

USERS

INTERIOR DESIGNE

The main users on this platform which place can show their design project, and they can interact their ideas on this platform. They have stronger professional skills to tell and help people decorate their home

CUNSUMER

They had higher income, and they also need a high pursuit of quality life. At the same time, they regarded that home is a private space where they spent most time in there. Therefore, they thought that home decoration is vital important for them. Then, they wanna have a platform to quickly find someone can help them design their home decoration.

Through this investigation and analyze, this design interaction platform still have a some special meaningful. It can help people find their favorite design style and interact their idea.
